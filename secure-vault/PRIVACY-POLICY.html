Privacy Policy for SecureVault

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

Privacy First: SecureVault is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

1. Information Collection and Use

SecureVault does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No Account Required: The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • No Data Transmission: Your passwords, emails, usernames, and PINs are stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics services to track your usage.
  • No Advertising: The app contains no advertisements or tracking pixels.

2. Data Storage

All data stored by SecureVault remains on your device:

  • Local Storage Only: All credentials are encrypted and stored in your device's local storage.
  • Encryption: Your data is protected using AES-256-GCM encryption with a master key derived from your PIN.
  • No Cloud Backup: The app does not automatically back up your data to any cloud service.
  • Export Control: You have full control over data export through the app's export feature.

3. Permissions

SecureVault requests the following permissions:

  • Storage/File Access: Used only for importing and exporting your encrypted data backup files. This permission is only used when you explicitly choose to export or import data.
  • Haptic Feedback: Used to provide tactile feedback for better user experience. No data is collected through this permission.

4. Data Security

We take your security seriously:

  • All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption.
  • Your PIN is hashed using SHA-256 and never stored in plain text.
  • The encryption master key is derived from your PIN using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations.
  • Data is only decrypted when you unlock the app with your PIN.

5. Third-Party Services

SecureVault does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage providers

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

7. Children's Privacy

SecureVault does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app can be safely used by users of all ages.

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored data within the app at any time.
  • Export: You can export all your data to a JSON file.
  • Delete: You can delete individual items or clear all data from the app settings.
  • Portability: Your exported data is in a standard JSON format that can be imported back or used elsewhere.

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. We do not have access to your data and cannot delete it remotely.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Open Source

SecureVault's code can be reviewed to verify our privacy claims. We believe in transparency and encourage security researchers to audit our implementation.
